This blog is a public notebook. I write it mainly **for myself** — to keep learning and to
remember. Writing things down forces me to understand them properly, and reading them back
later reminds me where I've been. If anyone else finds these notes useful, that's a bonus,
not the goal.

It mixes life and technical content, organized into five categories:

- **life** — life in Japan
- **japan** — living in Japan
- **opensource** — starting open source, and lessons learned from it
- **career** — career records
- **tech** — technical articles

Posts are written in Traditional Chinese, English, or Japanese — one language per post,
whichever fits the topic and my mood. Each post also carries free-form tags for
finer-grained topics.
